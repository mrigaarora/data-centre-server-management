HELP DOCUMENT FOR EPR PORTAL FOR BATTERY AND WASTE MANAGEMENT

OBJECTIVE:


EPR (Extended Producer Responsibility) Portal for Battery Waste Management, established by the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) in India. The portal is designed to grant registration to producers and recyclers/refurbishers of batteries under the Battery Waste Management Rules of 2022. The rules apply to all types of batteries and impose obligations on producers to meet collection and recycling targets. The portal aims to enhance accountability, traceability, and transparency in fulfilling EPR obligations. This provides information about the rules, registration process, and the role of the CPCB. It also includes a login form and a message from the Prime Minister of India regarding plastic pollution.

About CPCB

The Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB), statutory organisation, was constituted in September, 1974 under the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974. Further, CPCB was entrusted with the powers and functions under the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981.
